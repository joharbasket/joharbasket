# Johar Basket

**Johar Basket** is a user-friendly grocery delivery platform that allows customers to order groceries from the comfort of their homes. Our mission is to provide a seamless, efficient, and affordable way for people to shop for their everyday needs online, ensuring fast delivery and top-notch customer service.

## Features

- **Easy Browsing:** Users can explore a wide range of groceries by category, including fresh produce, dairy, meat, snacks, beverages, and more.
- **Secure Checkout:** Ensures secure payment gateways for easy and safe transactions.
- **Quick Delivery:** Fast and reliable delivery service within the shortest possible time.
- **User-friendly Interface:** Simple and intuitive UI for seamless shopping experience.
- **Order Tracking:** Track your order status in real-time, from the moment it’s placed until it’s delivered to your doorstep.
- **Promotions and Discounts:** Regular offers and discounts to make your grocery shopping affordable.
